About this site

PlayLotería.Online is 100% free and built to be used in video calls, classrooms, and offices.

This game was made in 2020 so everyone, no matter how close or far, could enjoy la lotería.

Faq

How many people can play together?

Currently up to 63 people can join the same game at the same time. Keep in mind, smaller games last longer and have a cozier, more intimate feeling.



What are the rules to la lotería?

This online version is designed to support a variety of house rules. But generally you have a caller who draws the cards and several people trying to match the cards to spaces on their own board. When they’ve marked the right spaces, they win.



How do I use this site?

First you need a group to play with. Whoever is drawing cards should be ready to share their screen, either over a video call or on a big screen everyone can see. Everyone else join on their own device by entering a code given out by the host.


[Detailed hosting instructions]
  • Click Host Game
  • Decide if you want to remove/add any cards from the deck
  • Choose what pattern players need to match to win the round
  • Make sure everyone you want to play with has your room number
  • When everyone is ready, click start and begin drawing cards
  • When someone thinks they won, click their icon to review
  • After declaring someone the winner you can either keep playing or restart the game from fresh

How do I remove players from my game?

Clicking on a players name will kick them from the game.



Can I remove certain cards from the game?

When setting up a game you can select which cards you want include in the deck, your selection will be remembered the next time you play from the same device.
